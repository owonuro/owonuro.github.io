About Page

Balogun Quadri Tunde , a graphic artist, a teacher and a developer. I started my journey in life as a graphic artist. I have worked in printing press, publishing house, and schools. Currently a computer teacher.

One of the major drive of my success in life is my ability to face challenges and profer workable solutions. In my few years experience as a teacher I have realised that not everybody is interested in how tech works but how can I use tech to solve my problem. If what you seek is solutions , idea , simple guidelines on how to get the best from technology without stress then you are in the right place. The goal of setting up this blog is to profer technical solutions in a simplified way.
