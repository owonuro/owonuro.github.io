FREE DATA ON AIRTEL AND MTN

I have come to realise that a lot of people are not taken the free and legal data available to them on Airtel and MTN networks in Nigeria serious. Some might see this as a very small data while others might claim its insignificant. What can this small data do? They say.

The truth! This data cap is significant and can mean a lot especially when you echust your paid data and you are unable to get data either through online or by loading recharge cards. What exactly am I talking about? I am talking about the daily data given to subscribers of Airtel and MTN by Opera. It is true that this data can only be used on Opera browser on any android phones. But you can use this data to do anything on this browser like open Twitter, Facebook, Google search, watch short videos, and most especially, you can use it to download. Yes! I mean dowload files from internet. Currently, I get 50mb daily and this means a lot to me despite my high data consumption.

Remember, It doesn't matter if you have data or not. You just need to use your opera browser on your phone to get this daily awoof.
