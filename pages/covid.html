CORONAVIRUS PANDEMIC


Covid-19 broke out in December, 2019 in China. On 11th March, 2020, the World Health Organisation declared Covid-19 as a pandemic disease.


Bad side

According to the World Health Organisation (WHO), as of April 3rd, 2020, there are 50,489 confirmed deaths of Covid-19 and we are still counting because its still spreading like wild fire on all continent.


Bright side

This disease can be prevented although its highly contagious.


How can we prevent it?

There are many ways to fight Covid-19 but the best practice recommended by WHO is social distancing. Social distancing means that you should give a distance of not less than 5 meters between you and the next person. You can also do the following to stay safe.
